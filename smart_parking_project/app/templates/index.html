How this works

  1. A fixed camera captures the lot; each parking space's pixel coordinates were marked once as spots.json.
  2. Every space is cropped into a small image patch.
  3. A ResNet-18 CNN, fine-tuned to classify patches as empty or occupied, scores every patch.
  4. Results are aggregated into the counts and map above and refreshed on demand.
